Tarihin Masarautar Muza

Takaitaccen Tarihin Muza

Tarihi ya nuna cewa asalin Muza garin Kabawa ne kuma sai da akayi sarakunan kabawa guda sittin (60) kamin zuwan Fulani. Ita daular sarakunan Muza ta kabawa an yi ta ne a tsohon garin da ake kira Kufai, kuma daga cikin sarakunan akwai wadanda suka dade bisa gadon mulki, akwai wanda ma ya shekara dari (100) yana sarauta.

Zuwan Fulani Muza

Lokacin da Fulani suka shigo kasar kabi, akwai wani matafiyi wanda yazo daga kasar Mauritaniya, ana kiran sa Ambar. Ambar ya haifi yara guda uku, Muhammadu Eggo da Modi da Janbuzu. Da yake diyan Ambar mayaka ne kuma makiyaya, Muhammadu Eggo yaje Muza, Modi kuma ya je Sanyinnawal shi kuma Janbuzu ya tafi Magacci.

Sarki Muhammadu Eggo

A lokacin da Muhammadu Eggo ya isa Muza, ya tarar da daular kabawa. Yayi yaki dasu kuma Allah ya bashi nasara akan su. A wannan lokacin, Moyijo ke sarautar Yabo. A wannan lokacin ne, a shekarar 1796, Moyijo ya nada Muhammadu Eggo a matsayin Bafillace na farko da ya fara zama Sarkin Muza. Haka kuma, Moyijo ya baiwa Muhammadu Eggo, ya sha fama da harin da kabawa ke kawo mishi, amma Allah bai taba ba su nasara ba, amma Allah bai taba ba su nasara ba.

Sarki Abdullahi Ta Mallam

Amina ta haifa wa Muhammadu Eggo dan da ake kira, Abdullahi. A lokacin haihuwa ne Allah ya yi mata rasuwa. Baiwar Amina da ake kira Tamallam, itace tayi renon Abdullahi, kuma saboda haka ne ake kiran shi Abdullahi ta Mallam. Allah yayi wa Sarkin Muza Muhammadu Eggo rasuwa a shekarar 1850, A wannan lokacin, yaki tsakanin Kabawa da Fulani yayi tsanani. Shi Kuwa Abdullahi a lokacin yana yaro bai kai jarumtakar mahaifin shi ba. Saboda haka ne ya baro Muza Kufai ya koma garin Torankawa a inda yaci gaba da sarauta. Dag agarin Torankawa Sarkin Muza Abdullahi ya sara garin Muza yanzu, kuma daga Torankawa yake zuwa Muza ko wace shekara saboda noma.Sarkin Muza Abdullahi ya rasu a garin Torankwa a shekarar 1906, a lokacin Sarkin Kabi Daudu.

Sarki Muhammadu Gidado

Bayan rasuwar Sarkin Muza Abdullahi , Sarkin Kabi Daudu ya nada dan Abdullahi mai suna Muhammadu Gidado a matsayin Sarkin Muza. Gidado ya hau halifar sarautar Muza a garin Torankawa. Muhammadu Gidado yana sarauta a lokacin da Turawa suka zo a shekarar 1909. A wannan lokaci kuma Sarkin Kabi Riskuwa ke Sarautar Yabo, a shekarar 1914, Sarkin Kabi Riskuwa ya kai Dangara Bawa Torankawa a matsayin sabon Sarkin Torankawa. Shi kuma Sarkin Muza Muhammadu Gidado aka maida shi MUza a matsayin Sarkin Muza. Saboda haka, Sarkin Muza Gidado shine Sarkin Muza na farko da ya zauna a garin Muza a inda take a halin yanzu.

Bayan Sarki Muhammadu Gidado

Da Allah yayi wa Sarkin Muza Gidado rasuwa a shekarar 1936, sai kanen sa, Muhammadu Sanin Kauru, ya gade shi. Shi kuma Sanin Kauru, kanen sa, Muhammadu Kanaranga ya gade shi a shekarar 1941. Abin lura anan shine, Gidado da Sanin Kauru da Kanaranga duka diyan Sarkin Muza Abdullahi ne. Sarkin Muza Muhammadu Bello, dan Sarkin Muza Muhammadu Sanin Kauru, ya gadi Sarkin Muza Kanaranga a shekarar 1961. A yanzu kuma, Garba Bello Yabo, jikan Sarkin Muza Kanaranga wajen uba kuma jikan Sarkin Muza Sanin Kauru a wajen uwa, ya gadi Sarkin Muza Muhammadu Bello a yau ranar Jumuâ€™a 23 ga watan goma sha biyu, 2011, wanda yayi daidai da 25 ga watan Muharram, Hijira Mazon Allah (SAW) 1433.
